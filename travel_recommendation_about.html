About Us

Welcome to Dans Travel Recommendations, your number one source for all things travel. We're dedicated to giving you the very best travel recommendations, with a focus on dependability, customer service, and uniqueness.

Founded in 2021 by Dan, Dans Travel Recommendations has come a long way from its beginnings. When Dan first started out, his passion for travel and helping other travelers drove him to do intense research and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online travel guide. We now serve customers all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the quirky wing of the travel industry.

We hope you enjoy our recommendations as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
